<h2>Tech Stack and Workflow</h2>
<ul>

</ul>
<h3>Key Features</h3>
<ul>
<li>Fully responsive design</li>
<li>Blog and Projects that are easily editable</li>
<li>Markdown files converted to HTML for blog and project content to avoid repetitive HTML/CSS work</li>
<li>Animated bird flying across the top page</li>
<li>Smooth parallax backgrounds and scroll effects</li>
<li>Contact form and newsletter signup integrated with Resend</li>
<li>Clean and simple UI, composed with v0.dev and Shadcn components</li>
<li>Custom domain set up through AWS Route 53</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>

<ul>

</ul>
</li>
<li>

<ul>

<li>Finalized minor styling adjustments and tested responsiveness</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
